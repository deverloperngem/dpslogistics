Welcome to DPS Logistics

DPS Logistics, headquartered in Kalamboli’s Steel Chamber, is a leading logistics company specializing in the transportation of containers and trailers (30ft and 40ft). We are committed to timely deliveries and unmatched customer satisfaction.

Our services span across Maharashtra and India, offering real-time tracking, safe transit, and experienced drivers. Whether it’s heavy industrial goods or time-sensitive shipments, our team ensures every consignment reaches on schedule.

With a decade of experience and a loyal customer base, DPS Logistics stands as a symbol of trust, safety, and excellence in the transport sector.

Welcome to DPS Logistics

DPS Logistics, located in Kalamboli's prestigious Steel Chamber, stands at the forefront of the logistics industry in India. Specializing in trailer and container transportation, we offer solutions tailored to a wide range of sectors including manufacturing, retail, and infrastructure development. Our fleet includes 30-feet and 40-feet containers capable of handling large and time-sensitive consignments.

With over a decade of experience, we focus on timely deliveries, secure cargo handling, and cost-effective service options that ensure our clients' peace of mind. We employ trained professionals who understand the nuances of freight management and traffic logistics. Whether it’s local delivery or cross-state transport, our network ensures smooth movement of your goods.

Technology is at the core of our operations. Our vehicles are GPS-tracked, enabling real-time updates and route optimization. This ensures not just timely delivery but safety and transparency in the entire process. We also provide a support team that keeps our customers informed about their shipments around the clock.

Our base in Kalamboli, a hub for industrial and transport businesses, offers a strategic advantage in reaching western and southern Indian states swiftly. We operate under the philosophy of "Commitment First," always focusing on customer satisfaction through operational excellence and responsive support.

With a track record of reliability and thousands of successful shipments, DPS Logistics is not just a service provider — we are a long-term logistics partner. Choose DPS Logistics for professionalism, technology-driven operations, and unparalleled reach in India’s logistics network.
